Soul Healing

    Aura

    What is an Aura? An Aura is your bodys energy field. There are seven layers to your Aura; Etheric layer, Emotional layer, Mental layer, Astral Bridge layer, Etheric Template layer, Celestial layer, Ketheric/Causal layer. The Etheric layer is the first layer of the aura after the physical body. It is conneced to the first chakra, the root. The etheric aura is the layer that corresponds to the physical life, feelings, physical pleasure, pain, health and wellbeing. The Emotional layer is the second aura layer which vibrates the frequency of emotions and feelings and affects emotional balance as a whole. The Emotional body is connected to the second chakra, the sacral chakra. The Emotional layer gives the opportunity to have desires, emotions, imagination and psychic abilities. The Mental layer is the third layer in the aura and it is connected to the solar plexus chakra. It facilitates our cognitive ability to know, have thoughts, beliefs, ideas and higher mental abilities. It also represents ego and personal power. The fourth layer is the Astral Bridge layer and it is associated with areas of expression of etheral, emotional and mental layer. It is connected to the fourth chakra, the Heart. It shows your capacity for conditional and unconditional love. The astral layer is also a bridge between the lower three layers and the higher three layers of the aura. Almost all healing energies come from the astral layer. The Etheric Template layer is the fifth layer of the aura. It represents the entire blueprint of the body that exists on this physical plane. It is connected to the throat chakra and includes everything you create on this physical level including your identity, personality and overall energy. Diseases can be monitored and treated in this layer and be passed down to the physical body. It is activated by sound and strengthened by expressing your truth and knowing who you truly are. The Celestial layer is the sixth layer of the aura and is associated with enlightenment. It is connected to the third eye chakra. It consist of the visceral level, where we have access to higher intellectual knowledge and wisdom. The Celestial layer carries a very strong and powerful vibration that expresses the relationship with God, the Creator and unconditional love. It is developed through raising awareness, personal growth and creative visualization. It can also be stimulated through meditation. The last layer of the aura is the Ketheric Template layer and is associated with the divine and universal consciousness. It is connected to the seventh chakra, the crown. This is the layer where we become one with the Universe. It refers to our current incarnation and holds all information about your soul and previous lifetimes. This is the strongest and most resistant layer of the aura.

    Chakras

    What Are Chakras? The Chakras are seven key points in the body where energy flows in and around the human body. Each chakra consists of key organs and nerves in your body and states of being as well. When energy becomes blocked in a chakra, it triggers physical, mental, and emotional imbalances that manifest in symptoms such as anxiety, lethargy, or poor digestion. The seven chakras work like a network and are consistently renewing energy in the body by reciving the energy of the food you eat, your physical activity, your environment and many other influences. The seven chakras are arranged from the base of the spinal cord to the top. The first three chakras relate to matter while the last three are about the spirit. The first chakra is called the Root chakra and it is located at the base of your spinal cord. The Root chakra helps you to stay balanced, committed to your work and energetic. It influences your digestive system, bones, teeth and sexual health. It affects your ambition and drive to move foward in your life. The Root chakra is known as the foundation of all the other chakras. The second chakra is located near your belly button or navel area. It affects emotions such as desire, pleasure, and sexuality. The Sacral chakra gives us the ability to feel compassion, have balanced emotions and be intimate with others. It is connected to the body's liver, kidneys, spleen and autoimmune system. This chakra also corresponds to your creativity, attraction and success. The third chakra is located above the Sacral chakra between the sternum and is called the Solar Plexus chakra. The Solar Plexus is associated with the color yellow. This chakra influences intellectual thinking, stamina, confidence, productivity. The Solar Plexus governs the upper abdomen, liver and stomach. When this chakra is in perfect balance you will experience good physical energy, digestion and a willingness to move foward. The fourth chakra is the Heart chakra and it is located in the chest. It represents unconditional love and is linked to the color green. This chakra connects the matter and the spirit together. The Heart chakra is responsible for emotions such as love, hope, trust, forgiveness and empathy. When this Chakra is balanced you can expect to feel a sense of inter-connectedness, a feeling of being complete and compassionate. The Heart chakra governs the heart, arms, blood, and lungs. It affects your feelings of social identity, your trust in people and your compassion towards people. The fifth chakra is the Throat chakra and it is located in the throat. It influences how you communicate and is represented by the color blue. The Throat chakra is responisble for learning, creativity, and truthfullness. It governs the throat, teeth, thyroid and mouth. It relates to taking responsibility and being accountable for one's actions. It also influences your ability to express yourself with honesty and confidence. The sixth chakra is the Third Eye chakra. The Third Eye chakra is located in the center of your forehead and between your eyebrows. The Third Eye chakra is represented by the color indigo and is associated with wisdom, self control and spiritual awareness. The Third Eye chakra influences our intuition and inner knowing. When this chakra is balanced it will help you think and focus on ideas. Clairvoyance, and higher levels of consciousness are associated with this chakra. It works closely with the throat and crown chakras. The last chakra is the Crown chakra and it is located at the top of your head. The Crwon chakra is linked to the color white and violet. When this chakra is balanced you will feel one with the universe. The Crown chakra is directly linked to brain functions and emotional thoughts. It encourages self-realization, divine love and selflessness. When this chakra is opened and balanced you will feel intune with the things that inspire you most and bring peace. All seven chakras are critical to your emotional and mental health. If one chakra is inbalanced it can negatively impact the other chakras.
